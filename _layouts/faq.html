For Companies

Still Willows is flexible for US and European time zones. Scheduling is discussed during the initial discovery call to ensure alignment with your team.

Workshops are short, practical group sessions, typically lasting forty five to sixty minutes. They focus on providing clear strategies that teams can apply immediately.

Topics commonly include:

  • Managing stress at work
  • Building resilience in demanding environments
  • Navigating change and uncertainty
  • Developing healthy habits for busy teams
  • Preventing burnout

Workshops can also be customised based on the specific needs of your organisation.

Privacy and Confidentiality

Yes. Everything shared in coaching sessions is private and confidential. Managers and HR do not receive information about who is using the service or what is discussed.

Leadership receives only high level, anonymised themes such as common stressors or recurring challenges. This allows organisations to make informed decisions without compromising individual privacy.

Once an organisation is onboarded, employees receive an email containing a booking link. This allows them to schedule sessions directly with the coach at times that suit them.

The booking process is simple, private, and self service. Employees do not need to go through HR or management to access support.

For Individuals

Yes. While Still Willows primarily partners with organisations, individual coaching is also available for people seeking personal support outside of a workplace arrangement.

Individual coaching can support stress management, finding balance, rebuilding routines, navigating transitions, emotional wellbeing, and sustainable habit building.

Those interested are welcome to book a discovery call to discuss whether this support is appropriate for their needs.
