What We Offer

Still Willows combines individual coaching, group learning, and leadership insight to support mental wellbeing at both personal and organisational levels.

1:1 Wellbeing Coaching

Confidential, one-to-one sessions that support employees with the emotional and practical challenges that affect their ability to function well at work.

  • Managing stress and emotional overload
  • Improving emotional regulation
  • Establishing healthier work routines
  • Maintaining work life balance
  • Preventing burnout
  • Navigating change and transitions

Team Workshops

Short, focused group sessions that provide teams with shared language, tools, and strategies for managing stress and working more sustainably.

  • Managing stress at work
  • Building resilience in demanding environments
  • Navigating uncertainty and organisational change
  • Developing healthy habits for busy teams
  • Preventing burnout before it escalates

Reporting & Leadership Insights

To support informed decision-making, organisations receive high-level insights into overall wellbeing trends within their team.

  • Anonymised themes observed across coaching sessions
  • Utilisation patterns
  • Practical recommendations for leadership and HR
  • Periodic trend summaries

What Teams Can Expect

Organisations working with Still Willows often see:

Reduced stress levels across teams
Improved emotional regulation and focus
Healthier work routines
Lower burnout risk
Improved morale and engagement
A more supportive and sustainable workplace culture

The focus is on steady, realistic improvement rather than short-term fixes.

How It Works

For Employees

Once your organisation is onboarded, employees receive an email with a booking link that allows them to schedule sessions directly with the coach at times that work for them.

Sessions take place via video call and typically last 45 to 50 minutes. Participation is voluntary, private, and confidential. Employees do not need to go through HR or management to access support.

For Leaders

Leaders receive regular check ins and high-level insights about how the team is doing overall. This may include emerging themes, areas where additional support could help, and practical recommendations.

At no point will leaders or HR know who is using the service or what is discussed. Confidentiality is a core part of how Still Willows operates.

Support for Individuals

While Still Willows primarily partners with organisations, individual coaching is also available for people seeking personal support outside of a workplace arrangement.

Individual coaching can support:

  • Stress and overwhelm
  • Rebuilding routines
  • Navigating life or career transitions
  • Emotional wellbeing
  • Sustainable habit building

Individual coaching is non-clinical and subject to the same Terms of Service and Privacy Policy as organisational coaching.
