How It Works

For Employees

Once your company is set up, each employee receives a private booking link. They can schedule sessions at times that work for them — no need to go through HR or management.

Sessions are held via video call and typically last 45–50 minutes. Everything discussed is completely confidential.

For Leaders

You'll receive regular check-ins and high-level insights about how your team is doing — themes we're seeing, areas where support might help, and recommendations.

You'll never know who's using the service or what they discuss. Privacy is foundational to how we operate.

Support for Individuals

Although Still Willows primarily works with teams, I also offer 1:1 coaching for individuals who want personal support — separate from any company plan.

Individual coaching can help with:

  • Stress and overwhelm
  • Finding balance
  • Rebuilding routines
  • Navigating transitions
  • Emotional wellbeing
  • Healthy habit-building
