The Programme

This is not a wellness app or a PDF toolkit. It is structured, human coaching delivered virtually, built around your team's schedule. Already running with one pilot organisation, we are opening a second spot now.

Confidential 1:1 Coaching

Private sessions available to every team member throughout the 12 weeks. Voluntary, scheduled at each individual's convenience, and available across all US time zones.

Pre and Post Wellbeing Survey

A short, validated wellbeing survey at the start and end of the programme. Anonymous responses, with results that show what genuinely shifted for the team.

Leadership Insights Report

An anonymised, high-level summary of themes and recommendations shared at the end of the programme. Individual privacy is fully protected at every stage.

What We Ask

In exchange for the full programme at no cost, we ask for honest feedback and, if the experience is genuinely positive, a written testimonial.

  • Encourage your team to complete the wellbeing surveys (participation is always voluntary)
  • A brief midpoint check-in and a wrap-up feedback conversation at Week 12
  • A written testimonial or short recorded reflection, provided the experience was a positive one
  • Permission to reference the partnership in anonymised case study materials

About Rizqa

I'm Rizqa, an occupational therapist and public health practitioner (BSc OT and MPH from UCT) with experience in mental health settings across Africa, Europe, and the United States, including a psychiatric hospital and a COVID isolation centre. Still Willows is non-clinical. This is coaching and wellbeing support, not therapy. I'm honest about that boundary and will always refer team members on if something needs more than I can offer.
