About Still Willows

Teams thrive when they feel grounded, supported, and emotionally steady. But too often, wellbeing support comes in impersonal packages that don't reflect the realities of people's lives.

I'm Rizqa Armien-Yang, and I created Still Willows to fill that gap. I'm a mental wellness coach with degrees in Occupational Therapy and Public Health (MPH), postgraduate training in Positive Psychology, and years of experience across community clinics, frontline services, and organisational health roles. I've also provided online counselling to clients across Africa, Europe, and the USA.

Through Still Willows, I offer small teams mental wellness support that's practical, sustainable, and rooted in real human experience.

No generic wellness apps. No complicated HR processes. Just grounded, personalised support for real people.
