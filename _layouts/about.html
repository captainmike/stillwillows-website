About Still Willows

Grounded support for real humans doing real work.

At Still Willows, we believe teams thrive when they feel grounded, supported, and emotionally steady.

Meet Rizqa

I'm Rizqa Armien, the mental wellness coach behind Still Willows. With a background in Occupational Therapy, Public Health, and online counselling, I've spent years helping people manage stress, rebuild healthy routines, and improve their emotional wellbeing.

I created Still Willows because I saw a gap: most small teams care deeply about their people, but don't have access to affordable, personalised wellbeing support. Corporate wellness programs often feel impersonal — apps, webinars, and hotlines that employees rarely use.

I wanted to offer something different: human-first support that feels genuine, accessible, and actually makes a difference in people's daily lives.

Now, through Still Willows, I support small teams who want to prioritise mental wellness in a way that's simple, sustainable, and grounded in real connection.

Background & Training

My approach blends clinical knowledge with practical coaching — helping people not just understand their stress, but build sustainable habits to manage it.

  • BSc in Occupational Therapy
  • Master's in Public Health (MPH)
  • Online counselling experience
  • Certified in Positive Psychology
  • Resilience training certification
  • Trauma-aware and culturally sensitive practice
Important note: Still Willows provides mental wellness coaching, not clinical therapy. Employees needing clinical support are guided toward appropriate professional resources.
