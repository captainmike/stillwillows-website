About Still Willows

Grounded support for real humans doing real work.

Meet Rizqa

I'm Rizqa Armien-Yang, the mental wellness coach behind Still Willows. With a background in Occupational Therapy, Public Health, and online counselling, I've worked across community clinics, frontline services, and organisational health roles, including managing a COVID-19 isolation centre in Cape Town during the pandemic. Across all these teams, I consistently saw how mental wellbeing directly shaped productivity, motivation, and resilience.

I created Still Willows because too many teams that genuinely care about their people don't have access to affordable, practical wellbeing support. Through Still Willows, I offer organisations mental wellness coaching that's sustainable, grounded, and rooted in real human experience.

Background & Training

My approach blends clinical understanding with practical coaching to help people identify what's holding them back and develop sustainable habits and strategies for managing workplace challenges effectively.

  • BSc in Occupational Therapy - University of Cape Town
  • Master's in Public Health (MPH) - University of Cape Town
  • Management of a COVID-19 isolation centre in Cape Town during the pandemic
  • Frontline clinical experience at a community clinic during COVID-19
  • Experience working within a psychiatric hospital
  • Online counselling experience with clients based in Africa, Europe and USA
  • Postgraduate training in Positive Psychology
Important note: Still Willows provides mental wellness coaching, not clinical therapy. Employees requiring clinical mental health treatment are guided toward appropriate professional services.
